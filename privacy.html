Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 February 2026

Plain English Summary

We collect basic personal information to provide quotes, deliver services, and improve your experience. We do not sell your data. We use trusted third-party platforms (hosting, analytics, AI tools) to deliver services. You can contact us anytime to access, update, or request deletion of your information.

1. Collection of Personal Information

The Daly Creative (ABN: 67 540 020 478) collects personal information necessary to provide digital marketing, web development, branding and AI-related services.

We may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number and business details
  • Information provided during consultations or project onboarding
  • IP address, usage data and analytics information via cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide quotes and deliver services
  • Communicate regarding projects
  • Improve services and website performance
  • Send marketing updates (with your consent)

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent or lease personal information. We may disclose information to third-party service providers only as necessary to deliver services.

These may include platforms such as:

  • Wix, WordPress, Squarespace, Shopify, Webflow
  • Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, GoDaddy
  • GitHub, Cloudflare
  • Google Analytics
  • AI platforms including Google Gemini, OpenAI and Claude

Where possible, we select providers with appropriate privacy protections. Data may be stored outside Australia depending on the platform used.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to protect your information from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our websites (including www.thedalycreative.com and timdaly.online) use cookies to enhance user experience and collect analytics data. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some functionality may be affected.

6. Accessing or Correcting Your Data

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you.

Contact: hello@thedalycreative.com

7. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates take effect upon posting to our website.
